I have finished the visiting in Hamlyn Centre. Now I am back to Shanghai again.

Currently I am PhD Candidate and a member of PAMI Lab, led by professor Jie Yang. Before that, I received my MSc. degree in Shanghai Jiao Tong University in June 2015 and BSc. degree in Xi'an Jiaotong University in June 2013. I'm interested in indexing and retrieval for natural images and medical images. I've published several papers on major journals on conferences including IEEE TMI, IEEE TIP, IEEE CVPR, IEEE ICRA, IEEE/RSJ IROS, ACM MM, ACM CIKM and MICCAI.
